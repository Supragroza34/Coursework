Program Rules

Here are the rules that participants must follow:

  • Be respectful to other participants and organisers.
  • Please do not engage in cyberbullying or any form of online violence.
  • Any more than 3 request to bans will lead the moderators to permanently terminate your account.
  • Please feel free to raise any concerns .
  • Remember to have fun and create a safe space for others.

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in consequences, including expulsion from the program.
